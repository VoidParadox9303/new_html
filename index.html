About SIM Registration

The SIM Registration Act requires all mobile users to register their SIM cards with their respective telecommunications providers. This helps enhance security and prevent mobile-related crimes.

Why Register?

Registration ensures your mobile number remains active and helps protect you from fraud and unauthorized SIM use.

What You Need

Prepare a valid government-issued ID and ensure your personal information is up to date before registration.

Registration Deadline

Make sure to register before the deadline to avoid deactivation of your mobile services.
